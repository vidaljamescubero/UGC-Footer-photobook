Paper With Purpose

The best photo books and albums start with time-tested paper options, thoughtfully selected for high print fidelity and less environmental impact.

We source ours from Mohawk, the longest-running family-owned paper mill in the nation. Their continuous dedication to responsible production processes, renewable energy, and recycled materials make them one of the most sustainable mills in the world.

Print, Perfected

True to our roots as a photographer-founded company, we choose press-printing to ensure rich, true-to-life results in your personalized photo albums.

Not only does press photo book printing render images in flawless detail, it also reduces environmental impact as compared to methods used by most photo book makers.

The process allows us to craft our own color profile on press, for a warm and pleasant aesthetic that's unique to Artifact Uprising books.

Beautifully Simple

Moment-marking shouldn't be stressful, so we made it beautifully simple (and simply beautiful).

Curated design elements and plug-and-play layouts bring drag-and-drop ease to building your book. Need a hand getting started?

We offer an album design service for wedding and milestone albums, plus resources and tutorials that can show you how to create a photo book you’ll return to often.

Different by Design

Materials matter, but design defines. By pairing meticulously sourced materials with a minimalist and modern design philosophy, we hope to preserve your stories with timeless albums that honor heirloom and everyday moments alike.

That means slowing down to spark joy in even the smallest of details, from the hand-marbled endsheets of our Signature Layflat, to the peek-through, soft-touch cover of the Color Series.
